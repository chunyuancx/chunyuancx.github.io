ABOUT ME.

Hey there, thanks for dropping by! My name is Branson Ang, a student at DigiPen Institute of Singapore currently studying User Experience and Game Design. Specializing in Game Design, I am extremely passionate in bringing ideas and concepts into fully functioning games. Focusing on the experience between the user and application, I love exploring different possibilities in design. Apart from being well versed in all aspects of game design, I also have a of subset skills ranging from 2D Art and Graphics Design to C# and Unity.

At a young age, I was captivated by the experiences and interactions game could provide. As time went on, I began delving into game design, observing how the world's systems would react to players and each other. It was this that influenced my design pillars, like how intuitive level design guides the player along without instructions or how something as subtle as rain would effect other system within a game. It is these small unconscious details that players tend to overlook really bring a world to life, immersing players in the game which captivates me and motivate my design.

As you probably already guessed, I love games! My favorite genres are, Real Time Strategy, Shooters and Role Playing Games to name a few. I also love history, strategy and digital art! Be it historical or fictional I love reading up on tactics, equipment and how the people in the era carried themselves. Practicing digital illustration and martial arts is something I love to do in my free time. Recently, I have been working on a series of short stories called the Jotun Empire with a friend, you can find out more about it on my projects page!
